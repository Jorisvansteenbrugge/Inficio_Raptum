Where does the name Inficio Raptum came from?

Inficio Raptum is latin. Inficio means "Poison", Raptum means "Trophy".
Especialy when you are trying to find usefull data(our trophy) about toxicity(our Poison), Inficio Raptum sounds very nicely and is related to the subject.
